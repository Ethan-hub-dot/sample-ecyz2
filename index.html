How to Make: Krispy Kreme Donuts

There are plenty of ways you can enjoy donuts, whether baked or cooked, in whatever flavour and texture of your favour. Krispy Kreme's donuts are renowned for their irresistable light, fluffy and sweet traits, as well as their classic nutmeg taste and sugar glaze.

This is a straightforward recipe that will show you how to recreate those iconic treats in the comfort of your home kitchen, probably without having to go a long distance out. Perfect for consumption any time of the day, whether breakfast or dinner, and worth sharing with your family too.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/4 cup warm whole milk
  • 2 1/4 tsp/1 pack yeast
  • 3 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup white suger
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 3 Tbsp shortening, melted or oil

Glaze

  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 - 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 - 5 tbsp warm milk/water

Step-by-step guide

  1. Combine the milk and yeast in a container, set aside for 5 - 10 minutes
  2. Mix the flour, sugar, salt and nutmeg in a seperate bowl
  3. Add the melted shortening and egg to the yeast mixture, then add to the flour mixture and stir for 5 - 7 minutes
  4. Grease a bowl and add the dough, cover with plastic wrap and let it rise for 1 - 2 hours
  5. On a work surface or board, sprinkle flour and roll out the dough to 1/2 inches thick
  6. Cut out donuts using a donut cutter and leave on a baking sheet to rise
  7. Fry donuts until golden brown on both sides
  8. Prepare the glaze by mixing all four ingredients
  9. Dip donuts in the glaze and drip off any excess
